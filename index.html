About HomeInvest

HomeInvest is an AI and data driven real estate appraisal, analytics, and information site. Our goal is to offer real estate data in a user friendly way to investors, home buyers, home owners, etc. so investing or just buying a home can be a simpler. HomeInvest offers a wide range of services including price estimates, estimated rental yields, growth estimates, and more. HomeInvest hopes that your investing or home buying experience is benefited with the use of our services.

Meet A.L.F.R.E.D

Alfred (AI Learning For Real Estate Data) is an AI programmed to help you on your real estate journey whether it be for investing, home buying, or other real estate activities. Alfred can do what no human can. He can look at millions of properties, transactions, and other data to see the patterns and trends. That means that he can see the details that we, as humans, miss. Alfred uses this information and insight to help you make the best descisions for your real estate investments.



Alfred has over half a billion property records at his disposal that span decades of historical data. This means that he can see how a property has done in good and bad times, what changes impacted the value of the property, and much more information. It is like have some of the best real estate advisers assisting you in all your real estate investments.

But Alfred is far more than just the data. He has powerful AI, Machine Learning, and Data Analytics that allow him to adapt to both the markets and to your specific goals. He gets better and better all the time at helping you make the best decisions possible. And he is with you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week; even on holidays! He is always there working hard for you.
